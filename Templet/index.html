📈 Stocks Trading — Advanced Rules & Strategy
Institutional-grade frameworks for equity markets

⚠️ Educational Purpose Only: The strategies below are for learning and research. Always backtest before live trading. Markets involve significant risk.

🎯 Entry Rules
✅ Trend Confirmation
  • Only trade in the direction of the primary trend (weekly chart)
  • Price must be above 50 EMA & 200 EMA for long trades
  • Use ADX > 25 to confirm trend strength before entry
  • Wait for higher highs + higher lows structure (uptrend)
⏰ Timing & Candle Setup
  • Enter on daily close or first 15-min candle above breakout level
  • Look for strong bullish engulfing / hammer candles at support
  • Avoid entries in first 30 min of market open (high volatility)
  • Best entry window: 10:00 AM – 12:30 PM IST (trend established)
📊 Technical Confluence
  • RSI between 45–65 (momentum zone, not overbought)
  • MACD bullish crossover on daily timeframe
  • Volume spike ≥ 1.5× average on breakout candle
  • Price near key support / demand zone from higher timeframe
🔍 Stock Selection Criteria
  • Select stocks above Nifty 200 (strong sectoral tailwind)
  • Relative strength vs Nifty50 must be positive (RS > 1)
  • Market cap ≥ ₹5,000 Cr for liquidity assurance
  • promoter holding > 40%, FII/DII buying in last quarter
🚀 Advanced Strategies
📊 Swing Trading (3–10 Days)
  • Buy at EMA crossover (20 crosses above 50)
  • Target: previous swing high or 1:3 R:R
  • Use weekly support/resistance as key levels
  • Exit 50% at first target, trail rest with EMA20
  • Best in trending markets, avoid sideways action
💥 Breakout Strategy
  • Enter on closing breakout above 52-week high
  • Volume must be ≥ 2× average on breakout day
  • Place SL just below the breakout candle's low
  • Target: measured move = height of consolidation base
  • Use Darvas Box method for base identification
🏦 Position Trading (Weeks–Months)
  • Use monthly chart for direction, weekly for entry
  • Enter after quarterly earnings beat + guidance upgrade
  • Trail stop with 40-week / 200-day moving average
  • Pyramid add on pullbacks to key EMAs
  • Hold as long as fundamentals + technicals align
⚡ Morning Gap Strategy
  • Trade stocks gapping up >2% on high pre-market volume
  • Wait for first 15-min candle to complete
  • Buy above first 15-min high if gap holds
  • SL: below first 15-min low
  • Target: Previous day high / weekly resistance
📐 CANSLIM Method
  • C – Current earnings growth >25% YoY
  • A – Annual earnings growth for 3+ years
  • N – New product, CEO, or 52-week high
  • S – Supply/demand (low float + high demand)
  • L – Leader in its sector (RS rating > 80)
🔄 Mean Reversion
  • Buy when RSI < 30 (oversold) in uptrending stocks only
  • Price must be ≥ 20% below 52-week high but in uptrend
  • Bollinger Band lower touch = potential entry zone
  • Target: Return to mean (20-day SMA)
  • Tight SL: 2% below entry / below recent swing low
🛡️ Exit Rules & Risk Management
🚫 Stop Loss Rules
  • Max risk per trade: 1–2% of total capital
  • Hard SL: below key support / swing low
  • Trail SL with EMA20 once in 10%+ profit
  • Never move SL further away from entry — only trail it
  • Time-based SL: exit if trade hasn't moved in 5 days
💰 Profit Booking Rules
  • Book 30–50% at 1:2 R:R (first target)
  • Move SL to breakeven after first target hit
  • Let remaining position run with trailing stop
  • Exit fully if stock closes below EMA50 on daily
  • Do NOT hold through results unless strong thesis
⚖️ Position Sizing
  • Formula: Position Size = (Capital × Risk%) ÷ (Entry − SL)
  • Max 10–15% capital in a single stock
  • Max 3–5 open positions at a time (beginners: 2)
  • Sector concentration: max 30% in same sector
🚦 Daily Loss Limit
  • Daily max loss: <3% of account → stop trading for the day
  • Weekly max loss: <6% → reduce position size next week
  • Drawdown >15% → go to paper trading for 2 weeks
  • Review all losers weekly to find pattern mistakes

⚡ Futures & Options — Advanced Rules & Strategy
Professional derivatives trading frameworks

⚠️ High Risk Warning: Futures & Options involve leverage and can result in losses exceeding your capital. Requires formal knowledge of Greeks, expiry dynamics, and margin management. Paper trade for at least 3 months before going live.

🎯 F&O Entry Framework
📌 Index Futures Rules (Nifty / Bank Nifty)
  • Trade only in direction of weekly trend (EMA 20 weekly)
  • Enter after 15-min opening range breakout is confirmed
  • Use PDH (Previous Day High) and PDL as key levels
  • Avoid entries between 9:15–9:30 AM IST (volatile opening)
  • Track global markets (SGX Nifty, Dow Futures) pre-market
📌 Options Buying Rules
  • Buy options only when IV Rank < 30 (cheap premiums)
  • Use ATM or 1 strike ITM options for directional trades
  • Never buy options expiring in < 5 days (theta decay risk)
  • Delta between 0.40–0.60 for balanced premium & movement
  • Risk only 2–3% of capital per options trade
📌 Options Selling Rules
  • Sell options only when IV Rank > 60 (expensive premiums)
  • Sell OTM options at least 1–2 strikes away from current price
  • Always hedge: use spreads (Bull Put, Bear Call, Iron Condor)
  • Target: 50% of premium collected → close and book profit
  • Exit immediately if option doubles against you (2× loss rule)
📊 Greeks Cheat Sheet
  • Delta (Δ): Speed of option price change vs underlying
  • Gamma (Γ): Rate of delta change — high near expiry
  • Theta (Θ): Time decay — enemy for buyers, friend for sellers
  • Vega (V): Sensitivity to IV — buy before events, sell after
  • IV Crush: IV drops after events → options lose value sharply
🚀 Advanced Options Strategies
🦅 Bull Call Spread

Moderately bullish, defined risk

  • Buy ATM Call + Sell OTM Call (same expiry)
  • Max profit: difference between strikes − net debit
  • Max loss: limited to net premium paid
  • Use when expecting moderate upside, not sharp move
🦅 Iron Condor

Range-bound neutral strategy

  • Sell OTM Call + Buy further OTM Call
  • Sell OTM Put + Buy further OTM Put
  • Profits when price stays within the range
  • Best used with IV Rank > 50, weekly expiry
⚡ Straddle / Strangle

Volatility play (before big events)

  • Straddle: Buy ATM Call + ATM Put (same strike)
  • Strangle: Buy OTM Call + OTM Put (different strikes)
  • Profit from large move in either direction
  • Enter before budget / RBI policy / earnings
🎯 ORB — Opening Range Breakout
  • Mark the high and low of first 15-min candle
  • Buy CE if price breaks above ORB high with volume
  • Buy PE if price breaks below ORB low
  • Target: 1:2 R:R minimum; SL: opposite ORB level
📐 VWAP Strategy (Intraday)
  • Long bias when price is above VWAP; short when below
  • Buy on pullback to VWAP + bounce confirmation
  • Combine with VWAP bands for dynamic S/R
  • Exit all intraday F&O positions by 3:20 PM IST
🔢 OI Analysis
  • Max Pain = strike with highest OI (price gravitates here)
  • High Call OI at strike = strong resistance zone
  • High Put OI at strike = strong support zone
  • OI buildup with price rise = bulls adding longs (bullish)
  • OI buildup with price fall = bears shorting (bearish)
🛡️ F&O Risk Management Rules
🚫 Hard Rules (Never Break)
  • Never trade F&O without a pre-defined stop loss
  • Max capital in F&O: 20% of total portfolio
  • Never hold naked short options overnight
  • Exit all weekly options by Thursday 2 PM (theta risk)
  • Stop trading if daily loss exceeds ₹5,000 or 2% of capital
✅ Best Practices
  • Use a Trade Journal — note every entry, exit & reason
  • Paper trade new strategies for minimum 30 sessions
  • Learn from top traders: mark support/resistance night before
  • Watch Nifty 50, Bank Nifty, India VIX daily pre-market
  • Review your trades every Sunday — win rate, avg R:R, mistakes
₿ Crypto & Forex — Advanced Rules & Strategy
24/7 markets — decentralized & institutional frameworks

⚠️ 24/7 Market Risk: Crypto markets never close. Extreme volatility (±20% in hours) is common. Use proper leverage control and never risk what you can't afford to lose. Forex is regulated; crypto is largely unregulated in India.

₿ Crypto Trading Strategies
🕯️ Wyckoff Method
  • Accumulation Phase: Price consolidates after downtrend — institutions buy quietly
  • Markup: Price breaks out — enter on Spring (shakeout) or first pullback
  • Distribution: Price consolidates at top after uptrend — institutions selling
  • Markdown: Price falls — short opportunities or sell all longs
  • Look for volume anomalies to confirm Wyckoff phases
🔗 On-Chain Analysis
  • SOPR > 1: Coins moving at profit — potential sell pressure
  • Exchange inflow spike: Whales depositing to sell — bearish signal
  • HODL Waves: Long-term holders accumulating = bullish base
  • Fear & Greed Index < 20: Extreme fear = historical buy zones (BTC)
  • Use Glassnode or CryptoQuant for on-chain metrics
📐 ICT Concepts (Inner Circle Trader)
  • Order Blocks: Zones where institutions placed large orders — act as S/R
  • Fair Value Gaps (FVG): Imbalance zones that price returns to fill
  • Inducement: Liquidity sweep above highs before reversal
  • Market Structure Shift (MSS): Change of character signal for entries
  • Trade during NY or London session for highest liquidity
🔄 Crypto DCA + Swing
  • DCA Strategy: Buy fixed dollar amount weekly regardless of price
  • Buy more aggressively when Bitcoin dominance > 55%
  • Altcoins rally when BTC.D drops — rotate profits into alts
  • Use weekly RSI < 30 as aggressive DCA signal for BTC
  • Always keep 20% in stablecoins for opportunities
💱 Forex Trading Strategies
📊 Price Action Trading
  • Use pure candlestick patterns: pin bars, inside bars, engulfing
  • Trade only at key support / resistance / supply-demand zones
  • 1H–4H for entries, Daily for direction bias
  • No indicator needed — price tells the story
🕐 Session Trading
  • London Session: 1:30 PM – 5:00 PM IST (highest volatility)
  • NY Session: 6:30 PM – 10:00 PM IST (major moves)
  • London-NY Overlap: Best time for EUR/USD, GBP/USD
  • Avoid Asian session for most pairs (low liquidity)
🌐 Fundamental Drivers
  • Follow NFP, CPI, FOMC, RBA, ECB decisions
  • Interest rate differentials drive long-term trends
  • Risk-on → sell USD; Risk-off → buy USD & JPY
  • Use economic calendar daily (Investing.com, ForexFactory)
🛡️ Crypto & Forex Risk Management
🚫 Leverage Rules
  • Beginners: Max 2–3× leverage; Intermediate: Max 5–10×
  • Never use max leverage offered by exchange
  • Higher leverage = smaller position size (keep risk constant)
  • Use isolated margin, never cross margin for new trades
  • Liquidation price must be >15% away from entry
💰 Capital Management
  • Never invest more than 5% of net worth in crypto
  • Keep BTC as 50%+ of crypto portfolio (anchor)
  • Take profits systematically on 50%, 100%, 200% gains
  • Use hardware wallet for long-term crypto holdings
  • Keep emergency fund separate — never trade it
